# About Me

Here is **Yingying CHA** [(查莺莺)].<br>

I am a PhD student at the University of Western Australia, supervised by [Prof. Grace Oakley](https://research-repository.uwa.edu.au/en/persons/grace-oakley/) and [Prof. Mark Pegrum](https://research-repository.uwa.edu.au/en/persons/mark-pegrum/)

Prior to the UWA, I have worked as Project Officer/Education Technology Coordinator in the [Global Institute for Emerging Technologies Equity Team](https://giet-equity.eduhk.hk/) at the Education Universit of Hong Kong, under the leadership of [Prof. LIM Cher Ping] (https://www.eduhk.hk/en/experts/professor-lim-cher-ping) from Oct 2022 to Feb 2026. 

I obtained my Master of Education (with Distinction) at the University of Hong Kong in 2022, graduated with the Dean’s Honours List.  

If you are interested in any aspect of me, I am always open to discussions and academic collaborations. Feel free to reach out to me at — **chayingying [at] outlook.com**
